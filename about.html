Who we are

ABEG is a team of five undergraduate Computer Science students at the University of Calgary. We aim to improve the quality of life for all students by providing new throughways for communication between students, and providing new ways to access some of the student resources already provided by the university.

Why it's important

The driving force behind ABEG is and always has been providing resources to help students maintain good mental health, as well as do everything we can to help those afflicted with mental health issues get the help they need. While mental health has become a more common concern in recent years, people afflicted with mental illnesses such as depression anxiety often have trouble taking advantage of them, as doing so can require them to step outside of their comfort zone. That's why we're building the bridges that allow people to access them from somewhere they feel safe.
